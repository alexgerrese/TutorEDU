TutorEDU

Description

TutorEDU is a website that uses .edu addresses to pair users with a tutor for assistance in a subject. Unlike services like Chegg or Coursehero, tutoring is personal and localized since both tutors and tutees are students at the same institution. Similar to services like Uber or Lyft, users both “work for” and derive the benefits of the application, serving as both the tutor and the learner.
